    About Patrick Mahomes

    Patrick Mahomes is a 30-year old American professional football player who plays at the Quarterback (QB) position for the Kansas City Chiefs in the NFL. He played college football at Texas Tech before being drafted by the Kansas City Chiefs as the 10th overall pick in the 2017 NFL Draft.

    Patrick Mahomes on Madden NFL 26

    On Madden NFL 26, Patrick Mahomes has an Overall Rating of 92 with Improviser Archetype. His best Passing-specific Attributes as Quarterback are Throw Power and Throw on the Run, both rated 97. Among his General Ratings, the most notable attribute is his Toughness, with a rating of 99.

    Madden NFL 26 Abilities
    Bazooka

    Quarterbacks with generational arms have the power to stretch any defense to its breaking point. When they enter the zone; their maximum throw distance is increased

    Dashing Deadeye

    Passers with this ability have perfect accuracy on all throws under 40 yards while running outside the pocket (except on high/low throws).

    Red Zone Deadeye

    Passers with this ability have perfect accuracy on all unpressured throws inside the red zone (except on high/low throws).

    No-Look Deadeye

    Passers with this ability have perfect accuracy on all cross-body throws up to 20 yards (except on high/low throws).

    Pass Lead Elite

    Passers with this ability have increased throw power on bullet precision passes (except on high/low throws).
